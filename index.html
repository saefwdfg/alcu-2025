WHAT IS ALCU X GAMES 2025?

ALCU X GAMES 2025 is an annual multi-sports and cultural event organized by the Association of Local Colleges and Universities (ALCU) in the Philippines. It serves as a platform for student-athletes and artists from various Local Universities and Colleges (LUCs) to showcase their talents, foster camaraderie, and promote holistic development. It was hosted by Tubod College (TC) in Tubod, Lanao del Norte. This page is to preserve the action, passion, and excitement of every game. I believe that every athlete deserves to relive those incredible moments through powerful imagery.

Moments of Glory

Captured moments from each day of the competition

Day 1

Delegates, officiating officials from across Region 10, marks the vibrant opening of ALCU x Games 2025 through proudly parading from Tubod College to Mindanao Civic Center.

Despite the rainy and cold weather, TCGC proudly performed their well-prepared Saludo, showcasing their pride and unity.

Day 2

The second day of the ALCU X Games 2025 starts off with athletics games held at TCGC.

Competitors from different institutions are showcasing endurance, speed, and determination as they aim for gold in this long-distance challenge.

Day 3

Throwing events were held on the third day of the ALCU X Games 2025.

Discus and Javelin were dominated by athletes from TCGC garnering gold on the said events.
